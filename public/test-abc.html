Advanced Features Demonstrated:

  • Standard voice numbers: Using V:1 and V:2 for clearer compatibility
  • Dynamic markings: mp, mf, fff
  • Articulations: tenuto, staccato, accent
  • Fingering numbers: !1!, !2!, !3!, !4!
  • Ornaments: Triplets, trills, fermatas
  • Repeats and endings: |: :|, |1 :|2 ||
  • Text annotations: "_cresc.", "_rit."
  • Mid-piece key changes: [K:C] and back to [K:Am]
  • Chord symbols: "Am", "E7"
